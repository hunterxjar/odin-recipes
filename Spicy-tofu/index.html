Spicy Tofu Jeletot

What is spicy tofu

Spicy tofu hot jeletot or indonesian fried stuffed tofu, traditional Sundanese snack made of crispy tofu stuffed with stir fry vegetable, served with chili or chili sauce.

ingredients

  • 10 pieces White tofu
  • 50-60 gram flour
  • 1 table spoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 table spoon baking powder
  • 1 Table Spoon broth powder (chicken/mushroom)
  • 1 tspsalt or according to taste
  • Enough water
  • 5 clovescayenne pepper or according to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic

Instructions

  1. Cut the white tofu into 4 dice each. set aside.
  2. Heat the oil. Mix the tofu with the dressing ingredients until smooth. Enter when the oil is hot (I divide it twice into the pan)
  3. For the sowing seasoning: chopped white and thinly sliced ​​​​cayenne pepper. Put it in the drain/drain then put the drain into the hot oil. Remove when the bottom is yellowish. Don't make the onions bitter later.
  4. Arrange the tofu on a serving plate then sprinkle the onions and chilies.
